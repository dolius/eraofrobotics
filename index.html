What this site is for

It exists to make robotics easier to judge, not just easier to admire.

Robotics is no longer only a technical curiosity. It is becoming an economic and operational question. That means people need more than wow-factor clips and vague promises. They need ways to think about sectors, use cases, deployment logic, labor pressure, and where the real traction is starting to form.

This site is built to help with that. The aim is practical understanding: what is happening, why it matters, and what you should look at next depending on whether you are an operator, founder, investor, builder, or just trying to get smarter.
