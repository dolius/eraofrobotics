03 — What this site is for

It exists to help you judge robotics, not just admire it.

Robotics is becoming an economic and operational question, not just a technical one. That means people need more than wow-factor clips and vague promises. They need ways to think about sectors, use cases, deployment logic, labor pressure, and where the real traction is forming.

Era of Robotics is built to help with that. The aim is practical understanding: what is happening, why it matters, what to read first, what to calculate next, and which brief to download when you want a sharper summary.
