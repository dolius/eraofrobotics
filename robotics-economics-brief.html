Robotics Economics Brief

Robotics gets interesting when the economics stop being imaginary. This brief focuses on the practical questions: is the workflow ready, how does labor compare with machine cost, and when does the payback story become believable?

Download PDFUse the tools

The core questions

  • Is the workflow repetitive and stable?
  • Is labor pain high enough to justify change?
  • Will maintenance drag erase the upside?
  • How fast can a deployment pay back?

What operators should avoid

  • Buying around hype instead of need
  • Skipping workflow analysis
  • Ignoring maintenance and integration costs
  • Confusing a demo with a deployment
